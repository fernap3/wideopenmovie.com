Cast & Crew

Masha Zabara
Masha is completing a double-degree in film production and cello performance at Bard College, NY. Originally from Minsk, Belarus, she is the first in her family to come to the United States and become an award-winning musician. Currently, Masha concentrates on screenwriting and directing, as well as soundtrack composition.
mashazabara.com
Gus Aronson
Gus Aronson is student at Bard college, studying Film and Photography. His passion for his craft has earned him recognition in the Mill Valley Film Festival, the All American High School Film Festival, and a Silver Key and Bronze key from the Scholastic Art and Writing awards.
Isabel Polletta
Isabel is a part time model for photographer Alex Papamitrou. She is a psychology major at Bard College and her research interests center on anxiety, relationships, and social media.
Diego Marquez
Aspiring filmmaker born in Caracas, Venezuela and based in New York. He has worked as a director, editor, actor and writer in many films of different genres, ranging from comedy and drama to thrillers and horror.
